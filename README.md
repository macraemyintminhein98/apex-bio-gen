# BioBoost AI

BioBoost AI is an innovative web application designed to help individuals and small businesses generate compelling and personalized Instagram bios with ease. Leverage the power of AI to create engaging bios tailored to your interests, desired tone, and personal brand.

## Features:

- **AI-Powered Generation**: Input your interests, select a tone, and let AI craft unique bios.
- **Copy-Pastable Output**: Easily copy the generated bios to your Instagram profile.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Simple and intuitive design for a seamless experience.

## Getting Started:

1. Visit the BioBoost AI website.
2. Enter your interests (e.g., hobbies, passions, profession).
3. Choose your desired tone (e.g., friendly, professional, witty).
4. Click 'Generate Bio' and proceed to a secure one-time payment of $9.99.
5. Copy your newly generated, personalized Instagram bios!
